Formative experiences

My experiences are varied and I believe a strong foundation from a young age where I was given the opportunity to organise, manage and lead large events and organisations has helped form me to who I am today.

So while some might not consider these to be "Professional Experiences", these are some experiences that give a better sense of my formative years and I feel are worth sharing.

Secondary school years at St. Patrick's School

  • Prefectorial Board - Executive Committee Member
    • Was incharge of organising, planning and implementing large scale school events from scratch including the school's open house.
    • Built relationships with other institutions and acted at an official representative at many inter school functions including investitures as the EXCO member incharge of liason.
    • Set standards, shared lessons and oversaw the conduct of the prefects under my charge.

  • National Cadet Corps (Land) - Master Sergeant & Unit Sergeant Major
    • Organised and oversaw the training, camps, conduct and standards of the over 100 person unit.
    • Represented Singapore in the JROTC Officer Leadership Course at the Citadel Military College, South Carolina, USA and completed the course with distinction.
    • Completed the Advanced drill course and was appointed as an NCC Supernumerary.

  • Athletics - Field Captain
    • Oversaw the training and administative aspects of the field throwing team.
    • Represented school at national competitions.

Pre-university years at St. Joseph's Institution International

  • Photography Club - VP
    • Co-founded the club and organised events and photography trips.
    • Taught introduction to photography for beginners.

  • Rock Climbing - EXCO
    • Organised training for the competitive climbing team.
    • Represented school at competitions.
